Public Speaking Series

The Public Speaking Series is designed to help you build a proper foundation as speakers, practice in front of an audience, and finally, learn how to continue exploring the intricacies of writing and delivering a strong prepared or impromptu speech.

Session Topic Area Time Duration Description
1 Speech Craft 1.5 hours Participants will get a chance to learn the basic theories about writing a speech that properly guides the audience.
2 Delivery 2 hours This session challenges participants to integrate their body and their voice into a written speech.
3 Practice Session 1 1.5 hours Participants are asked to write a short minute speech to present to the group. Others will learn how to provide effective feedback.
4 Impromptu Speaking 2 hours The remainder of the participants complete their speech practice. A mini session on delivering impromptus is provided.
5 Personal Coaching 1 hour Participants recieve a one-on-one coaching session to provide more in-depth feedback about their strenghs/areas of growth.
