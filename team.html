Who We Are

Extempra's mission is to help people speak with impact.

This mission emerged from two public speaking experts who came together and discovered their passion for communications training.

After training hundreds of clients, we confirmed that the best form of training must be highly interactive, practical, and custom-tailored. These principles are the foundation of Extempra's training philosophy.
